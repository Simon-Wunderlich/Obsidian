# $-x = not(x) +1$
## Proof
1. 
$x + not(x) = -1$
$$
\begin{matrix*}[r]
10010100 \\
+01101011\\
\textcolor{white}{\rule{4cm}{0.4pt}}
\\
11111111

\end{matrix*}
$$
$(11111111) \space overflows \space  to \space -1$

2. 
$not(x) +1 = -x$
