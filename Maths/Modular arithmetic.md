### Notation
$x \space in \space \mathbb(Z)_y=x \space mod \space y$

### Congruence
x is congruent to y mod m if 
$$
x \space mod \space m = y \space mod \space m 
$$ 
ie
$$
x \equiv y(mod \space m)
$$
ie
$$
m|(x-y)
$$
