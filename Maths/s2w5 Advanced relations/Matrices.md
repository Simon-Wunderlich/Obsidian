$$

### Dot product of boolean matrices
$$
\begin{matrix}
A = \begin{bmatrix}
1 & 0 \\
1 & 1
\end{bmatrix}
\\

B=\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 1 \\
0 & 0
\end{bmatrix}
\\
\\
A \times B =

\begin{bmatrix}
A_{1,1}\times B_{1,1} + A_{1, 2} \times B_{2,1}  & A_{1,1}\times B_{1,2} + A_{1, 2} \times B_{2,2} \\
A_{2,1}\times B_{1,1} + A_{2, 2} \times B_{2,1}  & A_{2,1}\times B_{1,2} + A_{2, 2} \times B_{2,2}
\end{bmatrix}
\\
=
\\
\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 1 \\
1 & 0
\end{bmatrix}
\end{matrix}
$$
