### Binary operators

| Operator              | Symbol | Boolean algebra symbol | Example        |
| --------------------- | ------ | ---------------------- | -------------- |
| **AND** (conjunction) | ∧      | . or blank             | p∧q, p.q or pq |
| **OR** (disjunction)  | ∨      | +                      | p∨q, p+q       |
| **NOT** (negation)    | ¬      | ‾                      | ¬p, $\bar{p}$  |
| **XOR**               | ⊕      | ⊕                      | p⊕q            |
