### Permutations

$$P(n,r) = \frac{n!}{(n-r)!}$$
$$= n\cdot(n-1)\cdot(n-2)\cdots(n-r+1)$$
$$n \space choose \space r$$
### Counting subsets
Order doesn't matter for subsets
$$\# \space of \space r-subsets \space from\space  S=\frac{P(n,r)}{r!}$$
$$=\frac{n!}{r!(n-r)!}$$
$$=\begin{pmatrix}n \\ r\end{pmatrix}$$

$$\frac{n \cdot (n-1) \cdots (n-r+1)}{r!}$$
